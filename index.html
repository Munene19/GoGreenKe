About Us

Our story

  • December 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Near the end of 2019 I(C.E.O GogreenKe) began delivering face-masks for my dear Mum as a side-hustle around Nairobi. Considering the rapid demand of face-masks due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the income was surprisingly significant and I decided to consider a biggerstep. The best part is we just happen to be enthusiastic about cycling and fitness and it's benefits to the eco-system since it cuts down on greenhouse gas emissions.

  • April 2020

    GoGreenKe is Born

    After a couple of calculated risks, we began incorporating package deliveries within our trails. The more attention we gained in the cycling community the more reliable riders we attracted to our company.

  • July 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    After establishing a formidable network, the company also began to attract partnerships and GoGreen deliveries Ke officially took off as an eco-friendly, speed dail goods delivery company
